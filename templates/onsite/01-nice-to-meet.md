# Nice to Meet You

> **When to use:** You just met someone at the trade show and want to send a quick same-day follow-up while the conversation is fresh.
> **Best timing:** Within 2-4 hours of meeting, or that evening
> **Expected response rate:** 35-50%

## Subject Line Options

1. `Great meeting you at [Show Name]`
2. `[First Name] — good to connect today`
3. `Following up from our chat at [Show Name]`

## Email Body

Hi [First Name],

Great meeting you at [Show Name] today. I really enjoyed our conversation about [specific topic you discussed].

As I mentioned, [Your Company] helps [Industry] teams [key benefit]. I think there's a real opportunity to [specific next step or value for them].

Would love to continue the conversation after the show. I'll follow up next week with some [resources/ideas/availability] — unless you'd prefer to connect again tomorrow at the event.

Enjoy the rest of the show!

[Your Name]
[Your Title], [Your Company]
[Your Phone]

## Variables

| Variable | Description | Example |
|:---|:---|:---|
| [First Name] | Their first name | David |
| [Show Name] | Trade show name | MWC Barcelona |
| [specific topic] | What you talked about | AI in network optimization |
| [Your Company] | Your company | Lensmor |
| [Industry] | Their industry | telecom |
| [Your Name] | Your full name | Alex Chen |
| [Your Phone] | Your mobile number | +1 555 123 4567 |

## Tips

- Speed matters — the same-day email has 3x the response rate of one sent a week later
- Reference something specific from your conversation to prove you were paying attention
- Include your phone number — trade show contacts often prefer texting
- Keep it casual and warm — this is relationship-building, not selling
