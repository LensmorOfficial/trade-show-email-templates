# Booth Visit Follow-Up

> **When to use:** Someone visited your booth and you want to follow up with relevant information.
> **Best timing:** Same day or next morning
> **Expected response rate:** 25-40%

## Subject Line Options

1. `Thanks for stopping by our booth at [Show Name]`
2. `[First Name] — the [resource] I mentioned at our booth`
3. `Good to meet you at booth [Booth Number]`

## Email Body

Hi [First Name],

Thanks for stopping by our booth at [Show Name] today. It was great chatting about [topic they were interested in].

You mentioned you're looking into [their need/challenge]. Here's [the resource/link/document] I mentioned that might help:

[Resource URL or attachment description]

If you'd like to dig deeper, I'd be happy to [schedule a demo / set up a call / send more details] after the show.

Either way, thanks for the visit — hope you're having a productive event.

Best,
[Your Name]
[Your Title], [Your Company]

## Variables

| Variable | Description | Example |
|:---|:---|:---|
| [First Name] | Their first name | Maria |
| [Show Name] | Trade show name | MEDICA 2026 |
| [Booth Number] | Your booth | Hall 14, D-23 |
| [topic] | What they asked about | exhibitor data analysis |
| [their need/challenge] | Their pain point | finding qualified leads before events |
| [Resource URL] | Link to resource | https://www.lensmor.com/demo |
| [Your Name] | Your full name | Alex Chen |

## Tips

- Personalize based on what they specifically asked about at your booth
- If you collected their info via lead scanner, reference the conversation not the scan
- Attach or link the specific resource — don't make them ask for it
