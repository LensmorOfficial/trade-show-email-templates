# Thank You

> **When to use:** Standard post-show follow-up to everyone you met at the event.
> **Best timing:** Within 24-48 hours after the event ends
> **Expected response rate:** 20-30%

## Subject Line Options

1. `Great connecting at [Show Name]`
2. `[First Name] — thanks for the conversation at [Show Name]`
3. `Post-[Show Name] follow-up`

## Email Body

Hi [First Name],

I wanted to reach out while [Show Name] is still fresh. It was great connecting with you and learning about [something specific about their work or company].

Our conversation about [topic] really stuck with me — especially [specific detail or insight they shared].

I'd love to continue the discussion. Would you be open to a [15-minute call / virtual coffee] sometime in the next week or two?

Thanks again for your time at the show.

Best,
[Your Name]
[Your Title], [Your Company]

## Variables

| Variable | Description | Example |
|:---|:---|:---|
| [First Name] | Their first name | Jennifer |
| [Show Name] | Trade show name | NRF 2026 |
| [topic] | What you discussed | retail analytics trends |
| [specific detail] | Something memorable they said | the challenge of unifying in-store and online data |
| [Your Name] | Your full name | Alex Chen |

## Tips

- Send within 48 hours — response rates drop 50% after one week
- The specific detail you reference is what separates this from a generic follow-up
- Batch-personalize: write the template once, then customize the [topic] and [specific detail] for each person
