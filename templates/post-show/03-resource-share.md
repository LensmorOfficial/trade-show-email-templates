# Resource Share

> **When to use:** You promised to send materials (case study, report, demo link, pricing) during the show.
> **Best timing:** Within 24 hours of the promise
> **Expected response rate:** 30-45%

## Subject Line Options

1. `As promised — [Resource Name]`
2. `[First Name], here's the [report/case study] from our chat`
3. `The [Resource] I mentioned at [Show Name]`

## Email Body

Hi [First Name],

As promised during our conversation at [Show Name], here's the [Resource Type] I mentioned:

**[Resource Name]**
[Resource URL or description]

The key takeaway relevant to [Company Name]: [one sentence explaining why this resource matters to them specifically].

Happy to walk through this together or answer any questions. Want to set up a quick call?

Best,
[Your Name]
[Your Title], [Your Company]

## Variables

| Variable | Description | Example |
|:---|:---|:---|
| [First Name] | Their first name | Kate |
| [Show Name] | Trade show name | Arab Health 2026 |
| [Resource Type] | What you're sending | case study |
| [Resource Name] | Title of the resource | How XYZ Hospital Reduced Lead Time by 40% |
| [Resource URL] | Link | https://www.lensmor.com/case-studies/xyz |
| [Company Name] | Their company | MedTech Solutions |
| [Your Name] | Your full name | Alex Chen |

## Tips

- This email has the highest conversion rate because you're fulfilling a commitment
- Send it fast — the longer you wait, the less they remember the context
- Always include a bridge to a next step (call, demo, intro)
