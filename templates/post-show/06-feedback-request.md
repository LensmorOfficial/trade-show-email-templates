# Feedback Request

> **When to use:** Requesting feedback from event organizers, booth partners, or collaborators after a show.
> **Best timing:** 3-5 days after the event
> **Expected response rate:** 20-30%

## Subject Line Options

1. `Quick feedback on [Show Name]?`
2. `[First Name], how did [Show Name] go for you?`
3. `[Show Name] debrief — 2 quick questions`

## Email Body

Hi [First Name],

Now that the dust has settled from [Show Name], I'd love to get your perspective on how things went.

A couple of quick questions:

1. What was the highlight of the event for you?
2. Is there anything we could do better if we collaborate at future shows?

Your feedback genuinely helps us improve. Happy to share our own takeaways from the event if that's useful.

Thanks for being a great [partner/co-exhibitor/organizer] — looking forward to the next one.

Best,
[Your Name]
[Your Title], [Your Company]

## Variables

| Variable | Description | Example |
|:---|:---|:---|
| [First Name] | Their first name | Ana |
| [Show Name] | Trade show name | Expo West 2026 |
| [partner/co-exhibitor/organizer] | Their relationship to you | co-exhibitor |
| [Your Name] | Your full name | Alex Chen |

## Tips

- Keep it to 2-3 questions max — long surveys get ignored
- This works well for building long-term relationships with event organizers
- Share your own feedback in return to make it a two-way conversation
